  • I'll Be Missing You

    The Dynamic Influence of Spread in First-Person Shooters

    This is a thesis I co-wrote and coded the test environment for. The purpose was to study the effects movement inaccuracy has on FPS gameplay. We had 28 participants conduct 420 completions of a target practice course(total) and collected 90,000+ data points to analyze FPS gameplay in this 69 page thesis.

    Read it here

    In short, spread makes players:

    • Stop more
    • Shoot more
    • Go closer

  • Maze Writer

    A maze generator that can write things!

    Uses the depth-first search algorithm with a recursive backtracker.

    Simply put, it wanders around until it gets stuck, at which point it continues wandering from the last point it wasn't stuck at. When no such point exists the maze is complete.

    Quite pleasing to look at, I'd say.
